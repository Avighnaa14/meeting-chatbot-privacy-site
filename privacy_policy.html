Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how the Meeting Scheduler Chatbot (developed by ARJ Private Limited) collects, uses, and protects user information when using our chatbot and Google Calendar integration services.

1. Information We Collect

When you connect your Google Account, our chatbot requests limited access to your Google Calendar through OAuth 2.0. Specifically, we collect:

  • Your Google email address (for identification and communication).
  • Access and refresh tokens (used only to check your calendar availability and schedule meetings).

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely to:

  • Check your available meeting slots in Google Calendar.
  • Automatically create, update, or cancel meetings when you request through the chatbot.
  • Send meeting confirmation emails to you and other participants.

3. Data Security

Your information is stored securely in a protected MongoDB database. Access tokens are encrypted and used only for the purpose of managing calendar events on your behalf. We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party.

4. Google Data Access

Our use and transfer of information received from Google APIs will adhere to Google API Services User Data Policy , including the Limited Use requirements.
